The Dacia Logan is a small family car produced jointly by the French manufacturer Renault and its Romanian subsidiary Dacia since 2004. 
It is currently in its second generation and has been manufactured at Dacia's automobile plant in Mioveni, Romania, and at Renault's plants in Morocco, Brazil, Argentina, Turkey, Russia, Colombia, Iran and India. 
It is also produced as a pick-up at Nissan's plant in Rosslyn, South Africa.
